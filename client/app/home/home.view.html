The Cutting Edge website hosts an online archive of over 1000 stone and metal tools with sharp edges including axes, knives and weapons of various historical periods. The objects are part of the archaeological and ethnographic collections of the Great North Museum : Hancock (Newcastle upon Tyne) and Tyne & Wear Archives and Museums (UK).

The archive, including high-resolution images of the objects and their cutting edges, intends to support teaching and research into ancient and historic tools and weapons from remote stations. It allows in particular the assessment of wear marks and other use traces by artefact specialists and use-wear analysts worldwide.'
